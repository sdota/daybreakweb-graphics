Daybreak Our Story What We Believe Vision, Mission, & Values Our Staff

Real people. Real God. Real Change.

At Daybreak, we do more than just talk about being real-- we're building a community where people can be who they are and where they are on their life's journey and know they'll be accepted.   For us, it's all about being real and coming to know the real God.  So everyone is welcome, no matter what. 

But we also know God has so much good in store for each one of us....

Daybreak Church started in November 1999 as a result of a dream and a vision for a different kind of church – a place where people could be real with each other and with God. Even our name reflects our dream. We think it paints a picture of hope and life because we believe that God is the creator of all life and the source of real hope, and that every day can be a fresh start with God.  We've always valued authentic community were Real People can meet a Real God and experience Real Change...

At Daybreak we think knowing what you believe makes all the difference. When you live on a solid foundation of truth, you’re better able to handle the ups and downs your life brings you. We believe that…

God is bigger and better and closer than we can imagine. He created and loves all people...

Our vision, mission, and values keep us focused on what God wants us to do in our church, our community and our world.  They're central to everything we do and who we are. 

We're committed to following God's call for His church at large and for us at Daybreak Church.  Our vision, mission, and values come directly from God's greatest commandment to love Him and to love people, and from Jesus' great commission to his followers to go into the whole world telling people about His love and grace for them.

At Daybreak, we are all about team. Our staff is committed to each other and to Daybreak's vision. We strive to be servant leaders, equipping and releasing everyone in our church family to become all God designed them to be as they serve God and serve people.
